## âš™ï¸ How to Use

1. Open [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/@me) in your **browser** (Not the Desktop App).
2. Go to the Channel or DM you want to wipe.
3. Click the **Trash Can (ðŸ—‘ï¸)** icon in the top right corner.
4. Click **"Get Author"**, **"Get Server"**, and **"Get Channel"** to auto-fill your details.
5. **Optional:** Check "Media Scanner" if you want to backup/review images first.
6. Click the **Red Delete Button** to start!
